About the App

Adrise is a secure, lightweight tool designed for developers to monitor their ad revenue and performance metrics on the go. By connecting directly to the Google AdMob API, the app provides instant access to earnings, eCPM, impressions, and CTR through an intuitive mobile interface.

Our priority is your data security. The app acts as a viewer for your AdMob data and does not store or transmit your sensitive information to any external servers.
