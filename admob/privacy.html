Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

AdMob Dashboard (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and professional data. This policy outlines how the App interacts with your Google AdMob data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App uses the Google AdMob API (specifically the admob.readonly scope) to retrieve and display your ad revenue and performance metrics. This data includes:

  • Estimated earnings (Today, Yesterday, Month-to-date, etc.)
  • Performance metrics (Impressions, eCPM, CTR, Clicks)
  • Account and App identification details (Name, ID, Status)

2. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our highest priority:

  • No Server-Side Storage: The App does not have a central server. All data retrieved via the AdMob API is processed directly on your mobile device.
  • Local Processing: AdMob metrics are only temporarily cached on your device for display purposes and are cleared when you log out or clear the app's cache.
  • No Data Transmission: We do not transmit, share, or sell your AdMob data to any third-party services or external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App connects directly to Google services. By using the App, you agree to Google's Privacy Policy. We use OAuth 2.0 to ensure secure authorization without ever accessing or storing your Google password.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account Settings.
